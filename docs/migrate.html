Migration guide

Escaping OpenClaw?

430K lines of code. 17K open issues. Config hell. If you're burnt out on the complexity, you're not alone — and your knowledge doesn't have to stay behind. Move everything over in under 5 minutes.

1

Export your OpenClaw memory

Run this in your OpenClaw workspace to dump everything it knows about you:

cat ~/.openclaw/workspace/SOUL.md 2>/dev/null
cat ~/.openclaw/workspace/memory.json 2>/dev/null
cat ~/.openclaw/workspace/AGENTS.md 2>/dev/null
2

Save your scheduled tasks

List what you had running so you can recreate them:

# In OpenClaw, ask:
"List all my scheduled tasks with their cron expressions"
3

Install cogent42

Two minutes, one command:

curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/cogent42/cogent42/main/install.sh | bash
4

Seed your knowledge

Paste your exported context into the first message. cogent42 extracts it automatically into persistent memory:

"Here's context from my previous setup: [paste your SOUL.md / memory dump]. Remember all of this."
5

Recreate your schedules

No cron syntax. Just tell it in plain English:

"Schedule a daily health check at 9am"
"Run my backup script every Sunday at midnight"
6

Done

No YAML. No config files. No dashboard. Just works.

Your knowledge builds automatically from here. The more you use it, the smarter it gets about your specific setup.
